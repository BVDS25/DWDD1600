About me

I'm a storyteller through my lens. Join me on this visual journey of capturing life's fleeting beauty.

I began my photography journey when I was just 13 years old. I would practice doing self-portraits and taking pictures of family and friends. Today I'm pursuing further college education in visual arts. Now my best friends and I started our photography small business where we can get the perfect smiles out of people in the most beutiful places in Utah.
